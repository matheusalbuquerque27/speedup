## üí° Pronunciation Tips

### Common Mistakes:
- **Thirteen** vs **Thirty**
  - Thirteen = stress on TEEN (thirTEEN)
  - Thirty = stress on THIR (THIRty)

- **Fourteen** vs **Forty**
  - Fourteen = fourTEEN
  - Forty = FORty (no "u"!)

### Practice These:
- 13 - 30
- 14 - 40
- 15 - 50
- 16 - 60
- 17 - 70
- 18 - 80
- 19 - 90
