### Slide 2: Prepositions of Transportation
**Rules:**
- **BY** + most transportation: by car, by bus, by train, by plane, by taxi, by boat
- **ON** + vehicles you can walk on: on the bus, on the train, on the plane, on the boat
- **IN** + enclosed vehicles: in the car, in the taxi
- **ON** + bike/motorcycle: on a bike, on a motorcycle
- **ON** + foot: on foot (walking)

**Examples:**
- I go to work **by bus**.
- She is **on the train** now.
- They travel **by plane**.
- We walk **on foot**.
