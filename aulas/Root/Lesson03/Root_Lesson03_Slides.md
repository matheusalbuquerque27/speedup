### Slide 5: Future with "Going to" (Plans)

### Slide 6: "Going to" vs "Will"
**"Going to"** - Plans already decided:
- I'm going to visit my grandmother tomorrow. (I already decided)
- We're going to have a meeting next week. (It's scheduled)

**"Will"** - Spontaneous decisions, offers, promises:
- I'll help you! (I'm deciding right now)
- I'll call you later. (Promise)
- It will be fun! (Prediction)

**For this lesson, focus on "going to" for plans!**

## üí¨ Practice (20 min)

### Activity 1: Invitation Role-play (10 min)

**Setup:** Students work in pairs, each gets a scenario card

**Scenarios:**

**Card A:**
- Invite your friend to a movie this Friday at 7 PM
- Suggest meeting at the cinema
- Ask if they want to have dinner after

**Card B:**
- Accept the invitation
- Ask what movie they're going to watch
- Ask who else is going
- Suggest a restaurant for dinner

**Card C:**
- Invite your friend to your birthday party on Saturday
- It's at your house at 6 PM
- Tell them what you're going to do (music, games, food)

**Card D:**
- Decline politely (you have to visit your family)
- Suggest meeting another time
- Wish them a happy birthday

**Switch roles and scenarios after 5 minutes**
