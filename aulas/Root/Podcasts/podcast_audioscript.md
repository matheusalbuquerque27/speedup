## Script

### Opening (30 seconds)

**Alex:** Hi everyone! Welcome to English Conversations. I'm Alex.

**Sam:** And I'm Sam. How are you today, Alex?

**Alex:** I'm great! Today we're going to talk about travel and trips. Sam, do you like to travel?

**Sam:** Yes, I love traveling! I enjoy visiting new places and meeting new people.

**Alex:** Me too! So, let's talk about our experiences.

---

### Main Conversation Part 1: Last Trip (2 minutes)

**Alex:** Sam, where did you go on your last vacation?

**Sam:** Last summer, I went to Rio de Janeiro. It was amazing!

**Alex:** Oh, nice! How did you get there? Did you fly?

**Sam:** Yes, I traveled by plane. The flight took about three hours.

**Alex:** That's not too long. Where did you stay?

**Sam:** I stayed at a hotel near Copacabana Beach. The location was perfect!

**Alex:** Lucky you! What did you do there?

**Sam:** Well, on the first day, I visited the Christ the Redeemer statue. I took many photos. The view was incredible!

**Alex:** I bet! What about the beach? Did you go?

**Sam:** Of course! I went to the beach every day. I swam in the ocean and played volleyball. It was so much fun!

**Alex:** That sounds wonderful. How was the food?

**Sam:** The food was delicious! I ate at different restaurants every night. I tried feijoada and brigadeiro. Yum!

---

### Main Conversation Part 2: Transportation & Plans (1 minute 30 seconds)

**Alex:** Great! Now, let me tell you about my trip. Last month, I went to São Paulo.

**Sam:** Oh, interesting! How did you travel? By plane or by bus?

**Alex:** I took the bus because it's cheaper. The journey took about six hours.

**Sam:** That's a long trip! What did you do in São Paulo?

**Alex:** I visited museums and I walked around the city center. I also met some friends there.

**Sam:** Nice! Did you buy anything special?

**Alex:** Yes, I bought some books and souvenirs. I spent too much money!

**Sam:** (laughs) That happens! So, Alex, do you have plans for your next trip?

**Alex:** Yes! I'm going to visit Bahia next year. I'm going to fly there and stay for two weeks.

**Sam:** That sounds great! I hope you have a good time.

---

### Closing (1 minute)

**Alex:** Thank you, Sam! Now, let's ask our listeners: Where did YOU go on your last trip?

**Sam:** Yes! Tell us: How did you travel? By car? By plane? By train?

**Alex:** And what did you do there? Did you visit any special places?

**Sam:** Think about it and practice your English!

**Alex:** Remember: Use past simple verbs like "went," "visited," "ate," and "stayed."

**Sam:** And use prepositions like "by plane," "by bus," "on the train."

**Alex:** Great job today, Sam!

**Sam:** Thanks, Alex! And thank you to our listeners!

**Both:** Bye-bye! See you next time!
