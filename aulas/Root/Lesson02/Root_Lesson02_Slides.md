### Slide 3: Simple Past - Sentence Structure
**Affirmative:**
- Subject + verb-ed
- I **worked** yesterday.
- She **played** tennis last week.
- We **visited** Paris last year.

**Negative:**
- Subject + **did not (didn't)** + base verb
- I **didn't work** yesterday.
- She **didn't play** tennis last week.
- We **didn't visit** Paris last year.

**Questions:**
- **Did** + subject + base verb?
- **Did** you work yesterday?
- **Did** she play tennis last week?
- **Did** they visit Paris last year?

**Short Answers:**
- Yes, I did. / No, I didn't.
