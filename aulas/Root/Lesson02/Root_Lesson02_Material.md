### 3. Simple Past Structure

**âœ… Affirmative (Afirmativa):**
- Subject + verb (past form)
- I **worked** yesterday.
- She **went** to the beach.
- They **played** soccer last week.

**âŒ Negative (Negativa):**
- Subject + **did not (didn't)** + base verb
- I **didn't work** yesterday.
- She **didn't go** to the beach.
- They **didn't play** soccer last week.

**â“ Questions (Perguntas):**
- **Did** + subject + base verb + ?
- **Did** you work yesterday?
- **Did** she go to the beach?
- **Did** they play soccer last week?

**Short Answers:**
- Yes, I/she/they **did**.
- No, I/she/they **didn't**.

### 4. Past of "To Be" (Was/Were)

**Forms:**
- I **was**
- You **were**
- He/She/It **was**
- We **were**
- You **were** (plural)
- They **were**

**Examples:**
- I **was** at home yesterday.
- She **was** happy.
- We **were** tired.
- They **were** in the park.

**Negative:**
- I **wasn't** (was not) at work.
- They **weren't** (were not) here.

**Questions:**
- **Were** you at the party?
- **Was** he sick yesterday?
