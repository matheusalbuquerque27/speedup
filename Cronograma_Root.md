# Cronograma SpeakUp - Nível ROOT
**Nível:** A1 Avançado a A2 Iniciante  
**Total de Lições:** 12

---

## Lesson 01 - Travel & Transportation
**Tema:** Viagens e transporte  
**Vocabulário:** Airport, ticket, bus, train, taxi, travel, trip, passport, luggage  
**Estruturas Linguísticas:**
- Review of Simple Present and Present Continuous
- "I'm going to..." / "I'm traveling to..."
- Transportation prepositions (by car, on foot, by plane)

**Warm-up:** Travel Bingo - Bingo com vocabulário de viagem

---

## Lesson 02 - Past Experiences
**Tema:** Experiências passadas  
**Vocabulário:** Yesterday, last week, ago, was, were, did, past, experience, memory  
**Estruturas Linguísticas:**
- Simple Past - regular verbs (-ed)
- Simple Past - irregular verbs (went, had, saw, did)
- Time expressions for past (yesterday, last night, ago)

**Warm-up:** Timeline Game - Criar linha do tempo pessoal e compartilhar

---

## Lesson 03 - Making Plans & Invitations
**Tema:** Fazendo planos e convites  
**Vocabulário:** Invite, plan, party, event, accept, decline, maybe, weekend, tonight  
**Estruturas Linguísticas:**
- "Would you like to...?" / "Do you want to...?"
- Future with "going to"
- Accepting/Declining invitations

**Warm-up:** Party Planning Challenge - Planejar festa em grupo rapidamente

---

## Lesson 04 - Food & Restaurants
**Tema:** Comida e restaurantes (avançado)  
**Vocabulário:** Menu, order, waiter, reservation, dish, appetizer, dessert, bill, tip  
**Estruturas Linguísticas:**
- "I'd like to order..." / "Can I have...?"
- Modal verb "would" for polite requests
- Restaurant conversation structures

**Warm-up:** Menu Speed Reading - Ler cardápio e fazer pedidos rápidos

---

## Lesson 05 - Describing People & Personality
**Tema:** Descrevendo pessoas e personalidade  
**Vocabulário:** Tall, short, friendly, shy, outgoing, smart, kind, personality, appearance  
**Estruturas Linguísticas:**
- Comparative adjectives (taller than, more friendly than)
- "He/She looks like..." / "He/She is like..."
- Physical and personality adjectives

**Warm-up:** Guess Who? - Adivinhar pessoa por descrição

---

## Lesson 06 - Home & Furniture
**Tema:** Casa e móveis  
**Vocabulário:** Living room, bedroom, kitchen, sofa, table, bed, furniture, rent, apartment  
**Estruturas Linguísticas:**
- Prepositions of place (advanced: between, behind, in front of, above)
- "There is/are" (review and expansion)
- Describing rooms and locations

**Warm-up:** Dream House Drawing - Desenhar casa dos sonhos e descrever

---

## Lesson 07 - Technology & Communication
**Tema:** Tecnologia e comunicação  
**Vocabulário:** Computer, phone, email, internet, message, social media, app, website  
**Estruturas Linguísticas:**
- Present Perfect (introduction): "I have used..." / "Have you tried...?"
- Technology verbs: download, upload, send, receive, connect
- Modal "can" for ability with technology

**Warm-up:** Tech Charades - Mímica de ações relacionadas à tecnologia

---

## Lesson 08 - Work & Professions
**Tema:** Trabalho e profissões  
**Vocabulário:** Job, career, office, salary, boss, employee, interview, work, profession  
**Estruturas Linguísticas:**
- "What do you do?" / "I work as a..."
- Present Perfect with "for" and "since"
- Job-related questions and answers

**Warm-up:** Profession Guessing Game - Adivinhar profissão por dicas

---

## Lesson 09 - Sports & Activities
**Tema:** Esportes e atividades  
**Vocabulário:** Soccer, basketball, swim, run, gym, exercise, team, play, practice  
**Estruturas Linguísticas:**
- "I like playing..." vs "I like to play..."
- Frequency adverbs (often, rarely, frequently, hardly ever)
- Sports verbs: play, do, go

**Warm-up:** Sports Pantomime - Imitar esportes para turma adivinhar

---

## Lesson 10 - Environment & Nature
**Tema:** Meio ambiente e natureza  
**Vocabulário:** Nature, tree, river, mountain, pollution, recycle, environment, animal, plant  
**Estruturas Linguísticas:**
- Modal "should" for advice and recommendations
- Environmental expressions: "We should recycle" / "It's important to..."
- Present Simple for general truths

**Warm-up:** Eco-Friendly Quiz - Quiz rápido sobre meio ambiente

---

## Lesson 11 - Culture & Celebrations
**Tema:** Cultura e celebrações  
**Vocabulário:** Holiday, festival, celebrate, tradition, culture, custom, special, ceremony  
**Estruturas Linguísticas:**
- Comparisons: "In Brazil, we..." vs "In the USA, they..."
- Cultural expressions
- Present Simple for habits and customs

**Warm-up:** Holiday Matching Game - Combinar celebrações com países

---

## Lesson 12 - Future Goals & Dreams
**Tema:** Objetivos e sonhos futuros  
**Vocabulário:** Dream, goal, future, hope, wish, plan, achieve, success, ambition  
**Estruturas Linguísticas:**
- Future with "will" vs "going to"
- "I hope to..." / "I want to..." / "I dream of..."
- Expressing future intentions and predictions

**Warm-up:** Vision Board Activity - Criar quadro de objetivos e apresentar

---

## Progressão de Aprendizado
- **Lessons 1-3:** Comunicação prática (viagem, passado, convites)
- **Lessons 4-6:** Situações sociais (restaurante, descrições, casa)
- **Lessons 7-9:** Vida moderna (tecnologia, trabalho, esportes)
- **Lessons 10-12:** Temas amplos (ambiente, cultura, futuro)

## Diferencial do Nível ROOT
- Estruturas gramaticais mais complexas (Present Perfect, comparativos)
- Vocabulário mais específico e situacional
- Maior foco em conversação fluente
- Introdução a tópicos abstratos e culturais
